The problem

DeFi is stuck at 150% collateral

Everyone must over-collateralize because no one can prove creditworthiness without doxxing their finances. VELA breaks the trade-off between privacy and trust.

🔒

Privacy vs. proof

To get better loan terms you must expose bank statements, balances and history. On a public chain that's unthinkable.

💸

Capital trapped

Fragmented identity
